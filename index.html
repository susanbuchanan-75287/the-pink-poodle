Where is The Pink Poodle located?

We're at 708 Stafford Dr, Princeton, WV 24740 — proudly serving Princeton, Bluefield, and all of Mercer County, West Virginia.

The easiest way is to text Britni at 304-921-2748 or use the booking form above. You can also call or email groomerbrit@yahoo.com. Britni will confirm a time that works for you.

What dog grooming services do you offer?

Small dog full grooms, baths & tidies, de-shed treatments, and gentle puppy first grooms — each finished with a signature bandana.

Do you groom large dogs?

Yes! While The Pink Poodle is known for small dog grooming, Britni and the team also groom medium and large dogs. Not sure if your pup is a fit? Text 304-921-2748 with your dog's breed and size and Britni will let you know.
