Meet Your Groomer

Hi, I'm Britni.

The Pink Poodle is my locally owned and operated pet salon in the heart of Princeton, WV — a bright, playful space built around one belief: your dog deserves the star treatment.

From nervous first-timers to seasoned show pups, I take the time to make every visit calm, gentle, and stress-free. Patience isn't a service here — it's the whole point. My own girl, Paris, keeps a watchful eye on quality control.

  • Small dog grooms tailored to breed & coat
  • Baths & spa refreshers that leave coats silky
  • De-shed treatments to tame the fluff
