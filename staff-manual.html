1. Signing in

Tap 🔒 Staff in the top-right corner and enter your PIN. Everyone gets their own 4–8 digit PIN from the owner. Your PIN decides what you can do:

RoleWhat you can do
StylistEverything day-to-day: the board, check-ins, notifying owners, checkout, clients, vaccinations.
ManagerAll of the above, plus void paid tickets, edit fees & the ledger, and edit the required-vaccine list.
OwnerFull access, including PINs, deposits setup, and the review booster.
Never share your PIN. If you forget it or think someone else knows it, ask the owner to reset it. Buttons you don't have permission for are hidden or will say "needs a manager/owner PIN."

2. The Status Board 🧼

The Board is your home base — today's pups shown as cards. Each pup moves along this pipeline, one tap at a time:

📝 Requested→ 🐾 Checked in→ 🛁 Bathing→ ✂️ Grooming→ ✨ Finishing→ 🔔 Ready for pickup→ 🏠 Picked up

On each card:

  • Next: … → moves the pup forward a step; ‹ Back steps back if you tapped too soon.
  • Filter by stylist using the pills at the top, and tap ↻ Refresh to pull the latest.
  • Badges show appointment time, deposit status, vaccination status, and 🔔 alerts on when the owner has turned on pickup alerts.

3. Checking a pup in

  1. Find the pup's card (or tap ＋ Walk-in to add one on the spot — just the pet's name and stylist).
  2. Tap Next: Checked in →. That's it — the owner can now see "Checked in" on their tracking page.
  3. Glance at the card for the owner's contact line and any 🆘 emergency / 🤝 pickup / 🩺 vet notes.
Once a pup is checked in, the owner-notification buttons appear on the card (see next section).

4. Letting the owner know 📣

After check-in, each card shows a row of notify buttons. Use them at drop-off ("checked in") and when the pup is ready.

ButtonWhat it doesWhen to use
✉️ Email: checked in / readySends the owner a friendly email right away. Works whenever we have their email.Drop-off, and again when ready.
🔔 Push: readyPops a notification straight onto the owner's phone.When the pup is Ready for pickup.
💬 Text (soon)Coming later — texting isn't switched on yet.Not yet — use email or push.
Push only reaches owners who turned it on. The owner has to tap "🔔 Alert me when ready" on their own tracking page first. If they haven't, the Push button will tell you so — just use ✉️ Email instead. Look for the 🔔 alerts on badge to know push will work. A * next to the Push button means the owner hasn't opted in yet.

You'll see a little confirmation ("✉️ Email sent" / "🔔 Alert sent to 1 device") when it goes through. If there's no email on file, add one in the Clients tab.

5. Finding owner & safety info

The card already shows the essentials: 📱 owner name · phone · ✉️ email, plus the pup's notes and any safety info the owner gave us:

  • 🆘 Emergency contact (name, relationship, phone)
  • 🤝 Authorized pickup — who else is allowed to collect the pup
  • 🩺 Vet contact for emergencies

For the full profile — all phone numbers, every pet, groom notes, history — open the 📇 Clients tab and search by name, phone, or pet.

6. Vaccination proof 💉

If an owner uploaded proof when booking, the card shows a 🔍 Vax proof button:

  • 🔍 Vax proof — opens the photo/PDF they sent.
  • ✓ Vax OK — mark it verified (you can save the rabies expiration date to their profile).
  • ✕ Vax reject — reject with a reason (e.g. "expired") so the owner knows what to bring.
No valid vaccination proof = the pup can't be groomed. Check this at drop-off.

7. Checkout & deposits 💳

  • 💳 Checkout — build the final total from line items, apply a discount or tip, pick the payment method, and record the sale. This posts to the books automatically.
  • 💳 Deposit — send/record a deposit for a future appointment (if the owner has it set up).
  • 🔁 Rebook — clone the visit forward as a standing appointment.
A paid ticket can't be deleted — it must be Voided (manager or owner) so the books stay balanced. Unpaid tickets can be removed with the ✕ button.

8. Cancelling, rescheduling & walk-ins

  • Cancel — cancel a ticket (with an optional no-show fee).
  • 📅 Schedule / Reschedule — set or change the appointment date & time; ✓ Confirm confirms it.
  • ＋ Walk-in — add a pup that arrived without booking.

9. Clients, Upcoming & Waitlist

  • 📇 Clients — every owner, their pups, contact info, vaccination status, visits & spend. This is where you add/fix an owner's email or phone.
  • 📅 Upcoming — future booked appointments.
  • 🎟️ Waitlist — pups waiting for an opening; tap to text the owner a slot has opened.
  • 💉 Vaccinations — who's due or missing proof.
